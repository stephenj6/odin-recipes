Pizza Recipe

Delicious Pepperoni Pizza Recipe!

This recipe was created by Beneditto Spatucci, originating in Milan, Italy.

He brought the recipe to New York City in 1956 when he immigrated and founded Spatucci's Pizza on 5th Ave.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients required to create Spatucci's Pepperoni Pizza

  • Dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella
  • Black Olives
  • Pizza Sauce

Steps

Follow this step-by-step guide to create this delicious pizza!

  1. Spread the dough out over a cookie sheet.
  2. Evenly spread the tomato sauce across the dough, covering every part.
  3. Grate the mozzarella, and then sprinkle it across the sauce.
  4. Slice the pepperoni, and then place each slide side-by-side across the entire pizza.
  5. Sprinkle black olives throughout the pizza.
  6. Place the cooking sheet in the oven and bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.
