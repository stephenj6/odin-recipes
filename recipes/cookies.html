Cookie Recipe

Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe!

Enjoy this incredible chocolate chip cookie recipe!

Ingredients

These are the ingredients required to bake these chocolate chip cookies:

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

Follow these steps to bake the cookies!

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
  6. Enjoy your cookies!
